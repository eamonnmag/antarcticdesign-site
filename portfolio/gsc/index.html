GSC Re-branding

The old logo looked like this:


The rebranding of the GSC logo was decided since the previous logo was a little old and outdated. The Genomics Standards Consortium board approached me to ask if I would redesign their logo to give it a fresh look. I based the new logo loosely on the old one (DNA theme) but drastically revamped the colour scheme, fonts and the DNA strand itself to make it look much better.
